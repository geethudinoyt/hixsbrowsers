Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hixs Browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a secure browsing experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser.

Important: Hixs Browser is designed with privacy as a core feature. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal browsing data to any external servers.

2. Information We Collect

Hixs Browser collects minimal information necessary for browser functionality:

  • Local Storage: Browsing history, bookmarks, and settings are stored locally on your device only
  • Cookies: Website cookies are managed locally and can be cleared at any time
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs may be collected for debugging purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing browser functionality (navigation, bookmarks, history)
  • Remembering your preferences (theme, search engine, etc.)
  • Improving browser performance and stability

4. Ad and Tracker Blocking

Hixs Browser includes built-in ad and tracker blocking features. When enabled, these features:

  • Block known advertising and tracking domains
  • Prevent companies from tracking your browsing activity
  • Improve page load times and reduce data usage

This is done locally on your device - no data is sent to any server for ad blocking.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • You can delete all browsing data at any time using browser settings

6. Third-Party Services

Hixs Browser may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Hixs Browser is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of Hixs Browser after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the browser's About Developer feature.
